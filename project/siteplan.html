Project Site Plan

Sofia Florylle S. Pantas

Overview

Site Name

"kamalayistudio.com" - This name represents a trio of artist from the Philippines

Site Purpose

The primary purpose of the site is to serve as a creative hub for the artists to showcase their diverse talents and collaborative efforts. The goal is to present each artist's portfolio, highlighting their individual styles and projects while also offering insights into joint projects. It should also provide an online space for selling artworks, prints, merchandise, or commissions, making it easier for consumers to support the artists directly.

Scenarios

"I'm interested in purchasing original artwork and prints. How can I browse and buy pieces from Kamalayi Studio?"

"I want to keep up with the latest news and projects from Kamalayi Studio. How can I follow the artists and stay updated?"
